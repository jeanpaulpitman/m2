Support The BRO Orchestra and Become a Benefactor

You are invited to become an Orchestra Benefactor and assist Townsville’s Community Orchestra with your personal and publicly acknowledged support which encourages others to do the same. With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we leave that up to you) you can become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. You can ofcourse remain anonymous if you desire.

Acknowledgment

The Benefactor roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months. As a benefactor you will also receive: Two complimentary tickets to our next concert, Exclusive VIP tickets to be a guest at our annual Season’s Launch function, and receive the BRO Monthly Newsletter.

BRO's charitable status

The North Queensland Ensembles Inc is a tax deductible fund listed on the Register of Cultural Organisations under Subdivision 30-B of the Income tax Assessment Act 1997. Donations of $2.00 or more are tax deductible.
