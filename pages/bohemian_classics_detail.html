Bohemian Classics, 2017

Pricing

  • Adults: $38
  • Concession: $34
  • NQE Members: $32
  • Student: $15

Intro

- including music by Dvorak, Brahms, Bartok and Enescu…featuring lively dances, a festival overture, work drawn from Romanian Folksongs and a symphony. Join us !

Program:

  • Antonin Dvorak: Slavonic Dance Op. 46 No. 8
  • Johannes Brahms: Academic Festival Overture Op. 80
  • Bela Bartók: Romanian Folk Dances Sz. 56.
  • George Enescu: Romanian Rhapsody OP. 11 No.1.
  • Antonin Dvorak: Symphony No.8 Op. 88

Conductor's:

  • Mario Dobernig
  • Mark Shiell
  • Concert Master: Stephen Frewen-Lord
  • Rehearsal Conductors: Suzanne Darrigan & Andrew Ryder
  • Orchestra Manager: Sally Frewen-Lord
