Major Sponsor

Barrier Reef Orchestra is supported by the Townsville City Council and gratefully acknowledges the generous support of its business partners and sponsors. The orchestra is run largely by volunteers and relies for its continued existence on corporate sponsorship and donations.

Corporate sponsorship packages can support general operations, a special project, a specific performance, an orchestral position, or a guest soloist. Sponsor benefits include advertising exposure, access to VIP tickets and special events. To discuss a sponsorship package for your business, please contact us today!
