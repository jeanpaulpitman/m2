Become a Member

North Queensland Ensembles Inc.(NQE) is the administrative body for the Barrier Reef Orchestra, by becomining a member you are supporting the Orchestra which was formed in 2000.

Membership Pricing

  • Adult: $30
  • Family: $60*
  • Full Time student or under 18: $15
  • Concession: $15**

*Family memberships avaliable to a group of two adults and three persons under the age of 18 all of whom reside at the same address. **Concession avaliable to persons in reciept of Centrelink pension card.
