PMI

Pluses:

  • We can now make websites!!!
  • Got to use great technology and software(Github, Phpstorm, Photoshop etc.).
  • Having multiple perspectives led to a better design outcome.
  • Having more people to share the load.
  • Learned to handle interruptions, setbacks and shifting goals.

Minuses:

  • Shifting goalposts, timing of content release's, and lack of clarity in assessment direction made coordinating the team activities difficult.
  • Using email instead of slack as the primary form of communication was a lost opportunity.

Interesting:

  • Having a colour-blind person provided unique testing perspectives for enhancing accessibility.
  • Unexpected things happening with Github (Merge conflicts etc.).
  • The difference in the design part of the planning as compared to the coding and structure parts.
  • User Testing highlighted the differences between the way a 'coder' thinks and the way the 'user' thinks.
